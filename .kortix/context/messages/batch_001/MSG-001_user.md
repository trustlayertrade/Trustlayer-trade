TrustLayer Trade – AI platform for trade document fraud detection

My name is Mohammed TAHIRI, and I am the founder of TrustLayer Trade, an early-stage Moroccan startup building an AI-powered platform designed to help importers, exporters, and trade-facing companies detect document fraud and critical inconsistencies before approving a transaction or issuing a payment.

International trade operations still rely heavily on manual review of sensitive documents such as:

commercial invoices,

bills of lading,

packing lists,

supplier banking details,

and supporting trade documents.

This creates exposure to document tampering, payment redirection fraud, inconsistent shipment records, and costly operational errors.

TrustLayer Trade addresses this challenge by automatically reading trade documents, extracting key information, comparing data across multiple files, detecting suspicious inconsistencies or alterations, and generating an explainable risk score to support human review.

Our initial target users are:

import/export companies,

freight forwarders,

finance and operations teams,

and SMEs involved in cross-border trade.

We believe this solution can offer significant value to internationally active businesses seeking stronger control over document validation processes while reducing manual risk and operational friction.
