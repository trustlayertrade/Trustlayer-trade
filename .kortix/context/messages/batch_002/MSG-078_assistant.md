Excellent idea! This is a **high-value feature** that would differentiate TrustLayer Trade. Let me break down the possibilities:

## **Company Verification Options (Feasibility & Strength)**

### **Tier 1: Basic (Easy to implement)**
- **Tax ID/VAT Validation** - Format verification + official registry lookup
- **Business Registration Check** - Verify company exists in trade registers (RC in Morocco, Companies House in UK, etc.)
- **Website/Email Domain Verification** - Check if email domain matches company website
- **Document Cross-Check** - Verify company name on invoice matches registration

### **Tier 2: Intermediate (Strong value)**
- **Trade References System** - Verified past trade partners vouch for reliability
- **Financial Health Score** - Integration with credit bureaus (Dun & Bradstreet, Creditsafe)
- **Sanctions/Pep Screening** - Check against OFAC, EU sanctions, UN lists
- **UBO Verification** - Identify Ultimate Beneficial Owners

### **Tier 3: Advanced (Premium feature)**
- **Real-time Bank Account Verification** - Confirm bank details match company
- **Insurance Backing** - Partnership with trade credit insurers (Coface, Euler Hermes)
- **Blockchain Trade History** - Immutable record of past transactions
- **AI Risk Scoring** - Combine all signals into "Company Trust Score"
