## 6. OBJECTION HANDLING

### "We already have a process"
```
"I understand. Most forwarders do.

Quick question: How long does your current verification take?

Our clients were spending 2-3 days per document. Now it's 30 seconds.

Even if you don't change your process, worth seeing if we can 
save your team time?"
```

### "It's too expensive"
```
"I hear you. Let me break down the ROI:

Manual verification: 2 hours × $20/hour = $40 per document
TradeSecurix: $25 per document (or less with plans)

Plus:
- One prevented fraud = $10k-$100k saved
- Faster processing = happier clients
- Compliance documentation = fewer penalties

What's the cost of one fraudulent shipment to your business?"
```

### "We don't have fraud issues"
```
"That's great to hear! May I ask how you know?

Many fraud attempts go undetected because they look legitimate.

Would you be open to a free fraud risk assessment? 
We'll review 5 of your recent documents and show you what we'd flag.

No obligation - just a second pair of eyes."
```

## 7. SUCCESS METRICS TO TRACK

### Weekly KPIs:
- **Outreach Volume:** Emails sent, LinkedIn connections
