# Wyoming LLC + Banking Setup Guide
## For Non-US Founders (Morocco/International)

---

## WHY THIS SETUP?

**Wyoming LLC + Stripe + Mercury + Grey = Perfect for SaaS**

- **Wyoming LLC**: No state income tax, privacy protection, $50/year fee
- **Stripe**: Best payment processor for SaaS
- **Mercury**: Free US business banking (no SSN required)
- **Grey**: Transfer money from US to Morocco easily

**Total Cost: ~$500-700 one-time**

---

## STEP 1: FORM WYOMING LLC

### Option A: Use a Formation Service (Recommended)

**Best Services:**
1. **Northwest Registered Agent** ($225 + state fees)
   - Website: northwestregisteredagent.com
   - Includes: Registered agent, LLC formation, EIN
   - Best customer service

2. **ZenBusiness** ($199 + state fees)
   - Website: zenbusiness.com
   - Good for basic formation

3. **Stripe Atlas** ($500)
   - Website: stripe.com/atlas
   - Includes: LLC + Stripe account setup
   - Premium option

### What You Get:
- Articles of Organization
- EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- Operating Agreement
- Registered Agent (required in Wyoming)
- Certificate of Good Standing

### Timeline: 3-7 business days

---

## STEP 2: OPEN MERCURY BANK ACCOUNT

**Mercury** = Free US business banking for startups

### Requirements:
- Wyoming LLC formed
- EIN number
- Passport (no SSN needed!)

### Apply:
1. Go to: mercury.com
2. Click "Apply"
3. Select "I have a US company"
4. Enter your Wyoming LLC details
5. Upload passport
6. Done!

### What You Get:
- US business checking account
- Virtual debit cards
- Wire transfer capability
- No monthly fees
- No minimum balance

### Timeline: 1-3 business days

---

## STEP 3: SET UP STRIPE

Once you have Wyoming LLC + Mercury account:

1. Go to stripe.com
2. Sign up as "Company" (not individual)
3. Enter Wyoming LLC details
4. Connect Mercury bank account
5. Verify business (upload Articles of Organization)

### What You Get:
- Full Stripe account
- Can accept international payments
- No volume limits
- Professional appearance

---

## STEP 4: SET UP GREY.CO

**Grey** = Receive USD in US, transfer to Morocco

### Why Grey?
- Get US bank account details (routing + account number)
- Receive USD from Stripe/Mercury
- Convert to MAD (Moroccan Dirham) or EUR
- Transfer to Moroccan bank account
- Low fees (~1%)

### Steps:
1. Go to grey.co
2. Sign up with Moroccan passport
3. Verify identity
4. Get US account details
5. Connect to Mercury/Stripe

### Alternative: Wise (wise.com)
- Also works well
- Slightly higher fees
- More established

## USING THIS FOR PAYMENTS

### Customer Payment Flow:
1. Customer pays via Stripe (credit card)
2. Stripe deposits to Mercury account (2-3 days)
3. You transfer from Mercury to Grey
4. Grey converts USD to MAD
5. Withdraw to Moroccan bank account (CIH, BMCE, etc.)

### Timeline:
- Customer payment: Instant
- Stripe to Mercury: 2-3 days
- Mercury to Grey: 1 day
- Grey to Moroccan bank: 1-2 days

**Total: 4-6 days to receive money in Morocco**

## TAX CONSIDERATIONS

**Wyoming LLC:**
- No state income tax in Wyoming
- Federal taxes apply to LLC profits
- If you're non-US resident: Only pay US tax on US-sourced income
- Most SaaS income = not US-sourced = no US tax

**Morocco:**
- Declare foreign income to Moroccan tax authorities
- Consult Moroccan accountant for details
- Keep good records of all transactions

**Recommendation:**
- Hire US accountant ($300-500/year) for LLC taxes
- Hire Moroccan accountant for local compliance
